Story

The year is 1498 and Japan is in chaos. The once peaceful village of Konoha has been overrun by the evil Akatsuki clan. The only hope for the people of Konoha is a lone shinobi ninja named Raikou. Raikou has been training for years to become a master ninja, and now it is time for him to put his skills to the test. Can Raikou defeat the Akatsuki and save the village? Only you can decide.
